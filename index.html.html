Welcome to RUMSO's Fit & Fun Challenge Day!

We're excited to invite you to an extraordinary community event that celebrates physical fitness, teamwork, and the joy of movement!

Our Mission

RUMSO is dedicated to promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and community engagement through the power of sports. We believe that staying active should be fun, accessible, and inspire people of all ages to live healthier lives. Through our Fit & Fun Challenge Day, we aim to create an inclusive environment where participants discover the joy of movement, build lasting friendships, and develop skills that extend far beyond the playing field.

What to Expect

Participants will rotate through 5 exciting challenge stations, each designed to test different skills while emphasizing teamwork and fun. Each station lasts approximately 5 minutes. The event begins with a group warm-up and concludes with a celebration ceremony where all participants receive recognition for their efforts.

Who Should Attend?

This event is perfect for anyone who wants to be active, try new challenges, and be part of a supportive community. All skill levels are welcome - our focus is on participation, effort, and having fun!
